Detailed Quick Commands for 2D AutoCAD

  • L - Line: Draw straight lines between two points.
  • C - Circle: Draw circles by specifying center and radius.
  • REC - Rectangle: Draw rectangles by specifying two opposite corners.
  • TR - Trim: Cut unwanted parts of objects.
  • EX - Extend: Extend objects to meet edges of other objects.
  • CO - Copy: Copy objects to a specified location.
  • MI - Mirror: Create a mirror image of objects.
  • AR - Array: Create multiple copies of objects in a pattern.
  • PL - Polyline: Draw connected lines and arcs as a single object.
  • DIM - Dimension: Add measurements to your drawing.
